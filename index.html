About me

I am an aspiring evolutionary biologist!

Diverse aspects of ecology and evolutionary biology excites me since my undergrad days. I am always motivated to do interdisciplinary research. Since I did a lot of field work in my undergraduate research and my ecological observations intrigued me to ask questions directed to evolutionary biology, I moved to such a lab during my MSc where I worked on different aspects of adaptation using experimental evolutionary paradigms of different model organisms. Most of my research till date revolves around phenotypic data which drives my interests towards the genome of organisms so that I can investigate the molecular aspects of evolution. Apart from ecology & evolutionary biology, I like to do photography and play musical instruments.
